# Pressure Tested — pressuretested.co

**Live URL:** https://pressuretested.co

## What This Is

Pressure Tested is a YouTube channel diagnostic platform for employed professionals over 40 facing AI displacement. It scores live channels using real analytics data and produces a ranked repair queue — not coaching opinions, not a course, not a sales call dressed as a discovery call.

## The Product

**Free Signal Diagnostic** — upload a YouTube Studio analytics export and receive a Signal Score out of 100, a breakdown across four signal layers, and a ranked repair queue in 30 seconds.

**30-Minute Diagnostic Call** — interpretation of the Signal Score for the client's specific channel, niche, audience, and professional background. Baseline established before engagement. Measurable delta after. Either the numbers moved or they didn't.

**White-Label SaaS** — the diagnostic tool licensed to YouTube coaches as a baseline instrument before Session 1. Contact: david@pressuretested.co
